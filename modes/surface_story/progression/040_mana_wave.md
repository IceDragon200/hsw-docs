# Game Mode - Surface Story - Progression - 040 Mana Wave

__Arc__ 4

__Tier__ Copper, Bronze

With the revelation of the mana plague that swept through Harmonia, as Nori finished his analysis a change takes place in the world requiring the player's attention back on the surface.

This arc introduces mana as a formal mechanic by enabling the world mana and mana corruption.

Players can now mine mana crystals throughout the world, which can be used to dive into various Harmonia features, regarding mana.

Some mobs will also drop mana related items upon death.

## Flow

* Mana Crystals are now minable
* Mana Crystals can randomly spawn in oversaturated blocks
* Mobs will drop some mana-related items
* Player will need to craft the "Mana Circuits" upgrade from crushed mana crystals
* Player will face Mana Blade which can randomly spawn to attack them once at maximum mana capacity.
  * If the player Defeats Mana Blade they will stop attacking them
  * If the player Survives Mana Blade they will stop attacking them
  * In either case, I9 becomes an NPC which can be found at the Celestial Tower from then on
* The player will obtain a copy of the "Blink Tome"
