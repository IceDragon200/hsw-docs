# Game Mode - Surface Story - Progression - 080 Sunken Cult

__Arc__ 8

__Tier__ LEA

After meeting a dead end with HOUND and the PAWNs, Nori laments the same problem when the player receives a mysterious message from an unknown party.

They attach several coordinates with a request to find them.

## Flow

* The player makes a visit to one of the Sunken Cult's Library facilities.
* The player meets the "Librarian", described as an apparition by Nori
* Player's gain the ability to replicate or make new tomes after mastering them
