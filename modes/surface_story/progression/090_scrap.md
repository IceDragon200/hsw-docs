# Game Mode - Surface Story - Progression - 090 Scrap

__Arc__ 9

__Tier__ Abyssal-Iron

With the threat of the Kavinu now a thing, the player has to manage their sleep schedule a little more closely to avoid provoking unwanted apparitions from appearing.

This Arc focuses on how Kavinu's spawn as well as the scrap mechanic and how to obtain Abyssal-Iron

## Flow

* The player receives another anomaly announcement from the Radio Tower, despite originally disabling it
* The player visits the Celestial Tower to find new areas unlocked.
* Player finds the tower's "Scrap Materializer" a magic device that uses the mana from the block to produce "Scrap".
