# Game Mode - Surface Story - Progression - 020 Grave Callings

__Arc__ 2

__Tier__ Stone

With the player now roughly up to speed with the basic gameplay features, they are finally given the reigns to explore the world.

No longer within the safety of the Celestial Tower, players are now outside and in the world exposed to the mobs and elements.

Grave Callings primary focus is on the boss mob: [Midnight Lich](/docs/lores/mobs/midnight_lich.md).

This arc primarily focuses on basic manual tasks, such as farming, foraging, mining and the crafting of medicine, linen fabrics and various pieces of armour or PPE.

With basic equipment all crafted up and equipped, players can finally tackle the Midnight Lich which grants them access to the Bio Lab for the Arc.

## Flow

* Introduction to Farming and Foraging
* Introduction to Stone Tools
* Introduction to Medicine
* Introduction to Linen Fabrics
  * Includes basic PPE and Armour
* Player can either gift an Apple or fight the [Midnight Lich](/docs/lores/mobs/midnight_lich.md)
* Player obtains the blueprint for [Bio Lab Access Card](/docs/lore/items/bio_lab_access_card.md)
* Player also receives the Bestiary function of their Codex
  * This also allows scanning entities with the Codex
