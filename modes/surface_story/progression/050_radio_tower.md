# Game Mode - Surface Story - Progression - 050 Radio Tower

__Arc__ 5

__Tier__ Iron

The PC receives an emergency message from Harmonia's "Emergency Services" system, advising that the world is now contaminated with mana.

Nori also hints that "Iron" is now mineable, whatever mysterious force that was preventing it has lifted.

The objective of this arc is to gain entrance into one of the many Radio Towers around the world, the tower was originally inaccessible as the doors were sealed shut and could only be removed with Iron Tools, which the player now has access to.

This is the only arc with no mandatory conflicts and is primarily focused on the player getting iron.

## Flow

* Player can mine iron and iron ores with their Bronze tier tools now
* Players are encouraged to get iron tools to gain access the Radio Towers
* Once in the Radio Tower, the player climbs to the top from the maintenance floor below it
* Nori informs the player that the source of the contamination is none other than the Celestial Tower
* The player returns to the Celestial Tower and checks the basement floor where a portal is revealed
