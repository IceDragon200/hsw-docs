# Glossary

* Abyss - A large space containing multiple domains and its own systems and structures overseen by a Primordial Will.
* AE - Aspect Element
* Aspect - Refers to the charge of a mana particulate changing its properties
* Aspect Element - AE, The energy form of mana, primarily with a charge to change its "Aspect"
* Blackstone - The laymans term for Kyrstone as it primarily appeared underground as a black stone that sparked upon touch
* Domain - Refers to a world or group of worlds typically overseen by a Zaonith, domains can be recursive so a domain can contain another domain.
* Element - Can refer to the Material Element, ME, or the Aspect Element, but primarily the former.
* Kyrstone - A form of MEMS, primarily found in the Wexkyr homeworld of Wexnia, highly sought after due to its purity, but is known to be difficult to refine
* Mana - The Abyss' primary energy and material source, mana takes many forms
* Mana Circuit - A series of secondary organs which appear in abyss dwelling life allowing the efficient transport and circulation of mana
* Material Element - ME, the physical form of mana, also just shortened to Element
* ME - Material Element
* MEMS - Mana-Element Mass State, also called ME, the physical form of Mana
* Nanosuit - A piece of equipment designed to enhance natural abilities with the help of mana
* PONA - Pilot-Operated Neuroadaptive Armour
* Syniso - Refers to several things throughout the eras, check the era specific to find out more.
* World - A portion of land or small space in which life can exist within the Abyss
* World Tree - An entity which connects directly into the abyss to regulate the flow of mana between the greater abyss and a World or Domain
* Zaonith - A higher authority who either created or simply manages a domain or group of domains, their ranks are called by Layer, where Layer 0 is the highest and subsequent layers are of lower rank, there is no limit on layers.
