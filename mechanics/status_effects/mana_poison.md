# Mechanic - Status Effect - Mana Poison

__Class__ Mana Slip Damage

Mana Poison seems innocent under normal circumstances, it slowly burns away any mana the entity has over time.

However, Mana Poison can inflict [Acute Mana Poison](acute_mana_poison.md) when the target's mana has dropped to 0 for a prolonged period which does not expire on its own.
