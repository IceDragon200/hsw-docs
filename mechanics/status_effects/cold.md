# Mechanic - Status Effect - Cold

__Class__ Movement Inhibition, Heat Adjustment

Cold unlike its heated counterpart doesn't immediately cause damage over time, but rather is a warning that the entity is susceptible to the "cold" family of effects.

For players, it's a warning that the environment is dangerous and can inflict [Frosbite](frostbite.md) if not careful.

Cold lowers movement speed and action speed and increases the stamina cost of running.
