# Mechanic - Nanosuit - Digestive Mana

**Era** Fracture

**Origin** Yuki

From one of the primordial devil's themself, you'd think this knowledge wouldn't be useful.

But seeing as your ability to eat is fairly inefficient, this should be useful.

Yuki was kind enough to explain the process of converting eaten materials into useful bits and bytes, though for your sake, it's more useful as actual resources.

Sadly Yuki's expertise didn't clarify how to extract more nutritional benefits from your diet.

## Effects

* + 0.50 (50%) Mana, Element and Energy from eating or consuming food items
* + 0.25 (25%) Eating Efficiency
