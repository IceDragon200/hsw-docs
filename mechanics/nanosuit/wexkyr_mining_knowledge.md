# Mechanic - Nanosuit - Wexkyr Mining Knowledge

**Organization** Wexkyr

A benefit of the Wexkyr's networked and deeply connected structure was their ability to quickly disseminate knowledge to all members.

The general mining techniques used to extract Kyrstone from the surface of Wexnia had proven quite useful to general material extraction.

Believe it or not it even comes in handy installable modules, like training wheels!

## Effects

* + 1 Mining Level (Applies to items and skills that utilize player mining rather than tools)
* + Unstable materials when mined by the player will not "detonate"
