# Mechanic - Nanosuit - GEE

**Era** Post-Paradox

**Creator** Gungnir

Gungnir Execution Engine (GEE).

Floating around the abyss, a copy of Gungnir's original execution engine used during the occupation of Lagrun.

It's missing some more personal touches, but the core system has been left intact, modified and fitted for your nanosuit.

## Effects

* + 1 Tome Level
