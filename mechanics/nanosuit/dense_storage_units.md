# Mechanic - Nanosuit - Dense Storage Units

**Era** Fracture

**Organization** FeXiN

Being former Wexkyr scientists and engineers, the Wexkyr now FeXiN are no stranger to breaking time and space "constraints".

The Dense Storage Units while originally adapted for the PONA had been miniaturized for general civillian usage and reinforced for military applications.

## Effects

* + 2000 Element Max
* + 2000 Energy Max
* + 10 Inventory Size
* + 10 Defence
