# Mechanic - Nanosuit - Abyssal Storage

**Era** Domain War, Fracture

**Organization** Jasmerine Industries

The Jasmerine family was widely known for its casual use of Abyssal Storage, starting fro its head down to the children.

When Jasmerine Industries was founded, one of their first products, was a book.

A simple guide to abyssal storage techniques and the how to, here it has been compressed down a module your nanosuit can formally understand.
