# Mechanic - Nanosuit - Self Repairing Suit

Restores the nanosuits active self-repair functionality, allowing you to recover up to 50% of your max health over time, it's not perfect, but it's better than nothing.

And no, it can't help you with your hunger.

## Effects

* Self-Repair function
* + 0.5 (50%) Self-Repair Max Rate
* + 0.01 (1%) Self-Repair Rate
