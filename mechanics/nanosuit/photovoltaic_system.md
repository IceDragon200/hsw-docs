# Mechanic - Nanosuit - Photovoltaic Systems

**Era** Paradox

**Organization** BULWARK

Now I know renewables were all the rage in humanity for a long time, but did they ever think to incorporate this direct energy gathering technique into their very bodies?

Of course not, they weren't running around in a literal suit of element.

But you my friend are, with some help from a few of my best co-processors we've developed a system adapted to your unique biology that allows you to sit in the sun... and gather energy.

## Effects

* + 2000 Energy Max
* + 10 Energy Regen while in light levels 10+ during the day
