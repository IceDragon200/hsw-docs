# Mechanic - Roller

The [Roller](../devices/roller.md) is a device used for shaping metals into other materials.

A [Die](#dies) shall be installed in the device to change the shaping profile of the Roller.

Dies can wear over time and dies may need to be upgraded to handle more difficult materials.

## Dies

* Rollers - Turns Lumps into Plates
* Press - Turns Plates into Cases
* Shredder - Turns Plates into Strips
