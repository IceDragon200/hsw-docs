# Mechanic - Magic Tome - Teleport

**AE** Void

**Level** 3

Due to its powerful effect, many domains monitor the usage of teleportation closely, and a zaonith is not afraid to step in to correct it.

Recorded in this tome however is the sanctioned usage of Teleport, requiring an additional catalyst to setup beacons that can be teleported to versus the free movement offered by its superior version.
