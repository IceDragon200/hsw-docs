# Mechanics

Mechanics are a list of functionality, resources or systems present in HSW some systems overlap or have multiple mechanics associated with them to form a complete package.

## List

Below are the list of mechanics in alphabetical order:

* [Abyss](mechanics/abyss.md) - The Abyss itself
* [BC](mechanics/bc.md) - BULWARK Credit, the currency for NPCs ingame
* [Breath](mechanics/breath.md) - Air, or breathing used when the entity is swimming or in an voided space
* [Clean Mana](mechanics/clean_mana.md) - First half of the entire Mana system
* [DATA](mechanics/data.md) - Message passing system
* [Dirty Mana](mechanics/dirty_mana.md) - Second half of the entire Mana system, and the bulk of the "system"
* [Domain](mechanics/domain.md) - A world within the Abyss
* [Element Crafting](mechanics/element_crafting.md) - Automatic, mostly free crafting system
* [EMP](mechanics/emp.md) - A status effect that specially affects electro-mechanical devices and entities
* [Energy](mechanics/energy.md) - General energy system utilized by electrical devices
* [Fluid Interface](mechanics/fluid_interface.md) - Primarily an API for allow nodes, entities and items to exchange fluids (okay, that sounded so wrong)
* [Heat](mechanics/heat.md) - Heat exchange system
* [Item Interface](mechanics/item_interface.md) - Similar to the Fluid Interface, but for items instead
* [Leather](mechanics/leather.md) - The process of acquiring, preparing and utilize leather, needed a whole document.
* [Magic Tome](mechanics/magic_tome.md) - Harmonia's bound system for magic usage
* [Mana](mechanics/mana.md) - Overview document of Mana
* [Message System](mechanics/message_system.md) - Story elements are disseminated over ingame messages via the message system
* [Metallurgy](mechanics/metallurgy.md) - The whole shebang of metal preparation
* [Nanosuit](mechanics/nanosuit.md) - Player nanosuit system
* [Node Clusters](mechanics/node_cluter.md) - The Node Cluster API, or how networks are formed
* [Nyctophobia](mechanics/nyctophobia.md) - Environmental hazard at low light levels during the night
* [Roller](mechanics/roller.md) - Overview of the metal shaping system, related to Metallurgy
* [Scrap](mechanics/scrap.md) - Overview of the scrap system part of the recycling systems
* [Spirit](mechanics/spirit.md) - Overview of the spirit system
* [Spirit Core](mechanics/spirit_core.md) - Spirit cores
* [Status Effect](mechanics/status_effect.md) - Overview of status effects that primarily apply to entities
* [Workbench](mechanics/workbench.md) - In-world crafting system
* [World Mana](mechanics/world_mana.md) - How mana works in the world at large
