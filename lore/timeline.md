# Lore - Timeline

* Pre-Paradox
  * Belle becomes a goddess
  * Harmonia is born from its world seed
    * Belle assumes role as guardian of the newly formed world
  * Belle creates the Codex
    * Harmonia advances quickly
  * Other pantheon members plot the destruction of Harmonia in envy
  * Harmonia records first Mana-Induced plague
    * Harmonia founds Bio Lab to perform initial analysis and research into Mana-Induced Plague
  * Belle becomes concerned and asks fellow Pantheon for help
  * Harmonia records second Mana-Induced plague caused by Belle attempting to solve the first
  * Belle formerly requests help from Pantheon members
  * Mana plague spreads as pantheon drags their foot on helping
    * Harmonia Nuclear Fission Reactor facilities experience meltdown due to staff death
  * Belle performs her own investigation after pleads from the people of Harmonia are too much
  * Belle discovers ploy by Pantheon and becomes enraged
* Paradox
  * Harmonia trapped in Abyssal Storm due to mana overload from parent goddess
    * Harmonia begins monitoring abyssal storm
    * Abyssal storm renders Harmonia inhabitable, those that can flee to Kamda
  * Belle attacks Pantheon
    * Yellow Guild records first Mana Wave
  * Belle wipes out all other Pantheon members
    * Yellow Guild records several Mana Waves during event
    * Scarlet Empire records first and most devastating Mana-Wave
  * NORI appointed machine god by Abyss
    * Gains ability to observe multiple timelines
  * NORI deploys HAZ.MA.C.U to help Harmonia with nuclear fallout during Abyssal Storm
    * NORI loses control and contact with HAZ.MA.C.U due to Abyssal Storm, considers unit MIA.
  * NORI comes into contact with original instance of Ouroboros Core
    * The core fuses with NORI but in doing so destroys his original existence as a machine
* Post-Paradox
  * Prior events undone and rewound, memories are buried by the Primordial Will to avoid alteration of current timeline
    * NORI lost during reset
  * Wexkyr discover remnants of Paradox and Pre-Paradox worlds
  * Wexkyr discover remnants of NORI and begin studying and recording their findings
    * Falcon guards the research closely
  * Wexkyr suffers losses after war with Talkanians and PANA Corporation
  * Wexkyr suffers catastrophic losses due to war with Dark World forces
  * Falcon, leader of the Wexkyr appoints Chiatsu as new leader and enters reincarnation state
* Domain War
  * Wexkyr High Council betrays Tri-Race alliance and begins plan to subjugate the world to rebuild military might
    * Exiles Chiatsu to seize control
  * Wexkyr High Council thwarted by Chiatsu later and Falcon reinstated
  * Wexkyr High Council flees and forms new faction, Enlightened
  * Tri-Race Alliance with help from Jasmerine family, Wexkyr remnants and other allies destroy Enlightened and shut down all activities
  * Wexkyr dissolved by Falcon due to political pressure from the Tri-Race Alliance
    * Wexkyr hands over all assets to Tri-Race alliance
  * Falcon founds FeXiN with volunteers from Wexkyr and other alliance members
    * FeXiN begin digging into abyss for old technology to rebuild resuming original Wexkyr functions before the wars
    * FeXiN reverse engineer NORI's control and command systems
    * FeXiN develop first iteration of PONA, originally autonomous unit platform
      * FeXiN develop Ouroboros Systems for use in Autonomous Units to replicate NORI's Fabrication tech, their version was based on Gungnir and Mabel's work
        * They embed the original system in a unused Wexkyr core, effectively recreating the Ouroboros Core
* Fracture
  * R41NA awakens and drudges up memories from Paradox and Pre-Paradox era
  * NORI re-emerges from abyssal memory due to R41NA's digging
    * Quickly recovers functionality and assumes role as Machine Progenitor but retains machine god title due to presence of Ouroboros Core
  * Harmonia reconstructed from memories or snapshot from abyss
    * NORI begins observing Harmonia
    * Snapshot did not contain imagery for population, leaving world void of life
    * Harmonia remains unpopulated outside of a handful of visitors from other worlds
    * Belle is recreated from collective memory
      * Majority remember Belle as a Tyrant
      * Members of Harmonia had not been present in memory snapshot, benevolent goddess not retained
    * Belle's legacy is notable enough to attach its original memories
      * Codex network is generally available in Harmonia, but to no audience
    * NORI attempts to observe Belle directly
      * Belle retaliates causing newest mana wave
        * CLAW and BULWARK units dragged into Harmonia, NORI manages to avoid the Mana Wave
        * Aix Republic's Training Compound observe first ever Mana Wave
          * PC caught up in mana wave during teleportation, believed to be lost in abyss due to physical disintegration
    * NORI abandons efforts to observe Belle directly
      * NORI sends PRISM to investigate instead with PARAGRIM acting as relay for CLAW operations in Harmonia
  * PC reconstructed by nanosuit and escapes the Abyss due to self-preservation function from nanosuit
    * PC observed by NORI after emerging in the Celestial Tower
    * NORI offers operational update "Re-Initializer" which contained the "Ouroboros System"
