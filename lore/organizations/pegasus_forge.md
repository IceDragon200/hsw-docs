# Lore - Organization - Pegasus Forge

**Era** Fracture

Emerging strictly out of a war torn Qarg Republic by former members of the annexed Dalar Kingdom during the Carbi PONA War.

Pegasus Forge was built strictly for advanced PONA construction during the war, with focus on the PONA and Pilot Link Field, despite being a arms company, reports say the engineers and workers were passionate about working with PONAs and treated them more as friends than products.

Outside of its reputation, it's leader Elliot Stone has been labelled as a war monger, actual evidence has yet to be seen...
