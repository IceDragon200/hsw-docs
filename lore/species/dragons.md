# Lore - Specie - Dragons

The apex of power and magic, yet almost always driven to extinction or near extinction in most domains.

Most surviving dragons are now descendants or have been influenced by the Dragon of Calamity, formerly known as Nidhogg.

And most dragons originate from the abyss primarily the Primordial Dragons that arose after the dissolution of the former Dragon of Calamity, it's power required multiple vessels to replace its original function.

That aside, dragons are known to be prideful, arrogant at times despite being near extinction.

However, those same dragons have been known to form deep bonds with those they come to love and trust, often sharing in their long lives with them, usually by a blood transfusion.
