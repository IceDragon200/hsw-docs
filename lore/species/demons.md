# Lore - Specie - Demons

Depending on the context, the meaning of "demons" change vastly.

When speaking of the Tri-Race alliance, it refers to the Demon-Kin, a sub-species of demons more inline with humans than their abyssal counterparts.

When speaking of the Celestials, they refer to the second-to-last stage of a fallen Celestial, a being that served a god of sort.

Sometimes Daemons are called Demons, but is a misnomer.

Ultimately demons are normally identified by the presence of large goat or ram horns upon their heads, a spaded tails, a penchant for magic and depending on the culture, can be deeply racist and prejudice, caste systems abound.
