# Aspect Element - AE

Aspect Element, or AE refers to mana in its charged elemental form.

## Aspects

Aspects are briefly covered in the Magic Lore section, but here is a list for reference.

They may appear in their latin names for some items showing its era.

__Neutral__

* Void

__Alignments__

* Light (Lux)
* Dark (Umbra)

__Fundamental__

* Fire (Ignis)
* Water (Aqua)
* Earth (Terra)
* Wind (Ventus)

__Extended__

* Lightning (Fulgur)
* Ice (Glacies)
* Metal (Metallum)
* Pressure (Pressura)

__Cross__

* Fire+Water = Steam (Vapor)
* Fire+Earth = Magma (Magma)
* Fire+Wind = Corrosion (Rosio)
* Water+Earth = Wood (Ligna)
* Water+Wind = Acid (Acidum)
* Earth+Wind = Dust (Pulvis)
