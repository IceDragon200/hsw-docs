# Lore - World - Domain - Wurfkale

__Appears In__ Surface Story

__Abyss Position__ Lower Abyss

__Class__ Hostile, Mana-Centric

Also known as the demon's pool or plain, Wurfkale is a realm no man should ever want to enter.

Wurfkale focuses purely on the magical aspect of the game, being home to the Abyssal Demons, it should be expected to be hostile.
