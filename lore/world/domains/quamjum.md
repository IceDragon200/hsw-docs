# Lore - World - Domain - Quamjum

__Appears In__ Surface Story

__Abyss Position__ Upper Abyss

__Class__ Hostile, Techno-Centric

Sitting above the natural furnace of Pyrotha, a civilization once thrived in Quamjum, now nothing but the remains of workshops of magicians and engineers alike litter the landscape.

Old constructs belived to have been defunct roam and attack everything in an attempt to stabilize their broken world.
