# Lore - World - Domain - Pyrotha

__Appears In__ Surface Story

__Abyss Position__ Upper Abyss

__Class__ Hostile, Environmentally Hostile, High Temperature

Heat, magma chambers, Pyrotha is home to condensed heat and mana stones, one should be prepared before entering this domain.

Pyrotha is the players first introduction to the influence of the abyss being at the upper abyss, Pyrotha provides access to lava, various rare and hard to find metals in abundance, if you can even reach them.
