# Lore - World - Domain - Harmonia

__Appears In__ Surface Story

__Abyss Position__ Surface

__Class__ Neutral, Mildly Hostile, Environmentally Hostile, Mana-Centric, Techno-Centric

Harmonia is surface world and where the player will likely want to be and the only place respawn points can be set.

## Classes Explanation

To explain its classes, it's considered a "Neutral" domain, which allows the player to set a respawn point in that world.

It is mildly hostile as there are entities which may attempt to attack the player.

It is environmentally hostile due to the presence of radioactive materials.

It does have mana and related mana features or mechanics as a focus.

It is also technology focused, being the default world.
