# Lore - World - Domain - The Dredge

__Appears In__ Surface Story

__Abyss Position__ Lower Abyss

__Class__ Hostile, Mana-Centric, Environmentally Hostile

Rather than a name, it was a description, one of the lower parts of the abyss, but not its lowest.

The abyssal horror is far too scary for a mortal to venture further than the dredge.

The remains of dead worlds that fell to the bottom of the abyss live here.

Live might be a strong word for the decay and destruction present.
