# Lore - Character - Avione

Better known as the [Midnight Lich](/lore/mobs/midnight_lich.md).

Avione was an old researcher working in [Harmonia's Bio Laboratory](/lore/world/domains/harmonia/structures/bio_lab.md), after the worst [Abyssal Storm](/lore/events/harmonia_abyssal_storm.md) in history, Avione's physical body had been torn apart but their spirit became trapped in Harmonia, suffering a fate similar to the PC, they had been barely regenerated when their lab coat fused with their remains.

Avione was one of the few scientists who was a trained magician having served in the army for several years before leaving to pursue biological research.
