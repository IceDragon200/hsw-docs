# Lore - Character - I9

Better known as the [Mana Blade](/docs/lore/mobs/mana_blade.md).

I9 was a humanoid drone created to execute violent mana plague victims.

As he was constructed near the end of the world, he was equipped with the Maphir's understanding of mana at the time.

A single Blink Tome was given to I9 allowing it to warp rapidly making it an effective assassin.
