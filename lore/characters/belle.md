# Lore - Character - Belle

The mana goddess, the grudge, Belle went by many titles, but the people of Harmonia remembered her as a kind goddess who blessed them with knowledge.

Even unto death, her people continued to believe in her.

Due to Belle's overwhelming power and support, even without active worshippers she remained active and untouchable.
